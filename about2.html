About Me

Profesionally Personally

Hi. Hello! What's Up?

My name is Danielle. When I'm not designing I'm usually
crafting or finding a new hobby to keep me busy. I love being
creative and doing anything where I can tell a story. Painting,
both traditionally and digitally, is one of the ways I express
myself. Most recently I've gotten into bookbinding. I love making
my own books and giving them to other poeple as gifts. There's
just something about taking a bunch of nothings and turn it into
something completely different.

Outside of being creative I like playing video games and reading
and taking naps. I love to travel and see different places and
learn about different people and cultures. My curiosity is always
pushing to learn new things. Right now I'm interested in learning
other languages. My goal is to be able to speak in at least seven
different languages other than Enlgish. I never want to stop
learning and being curious about the things I don't know.

Curiosity killed the cat. Satisfaction brought it back.
