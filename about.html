Hi. Hello! What's Up?

I've always been interested in anything art and computer
related. After studying Computer Science for two years,
I graduated from University of Texas at San Antonio with a
BA in Art. I soon decided that wasn't enough for me and less
than a year later I enrolled at Sam Houston State University
and graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design in 2022. Throughout
my time, I've learned that creativity allows us to be open-minded
and solve problems. My skills and personality have brought me
success, but I've always been interested in trying new things.

Since graduating, I've designed logos for a few local companies.
I've also worked a Graphic Designer for Red Table Talk and
Marketing Specialist for the Houston Food Bank. These
opportunities have allowed me to approach a variety of projects
and concepts with versatility and adaptability.

Creating is what I live for.
